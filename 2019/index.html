SemStats, 2019 edition

This year, SemStats is again co-located with ISWC and thus will take place in Auckland, New Zealand, on the 27th of October 2019.

2019 continues to be a year of important opportunities for SemStats with important projects combining statistics and semantics being launched by prominent international organizations: see the call for contributions for details.
